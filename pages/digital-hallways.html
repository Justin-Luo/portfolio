Digital Hallways   

Encouraging online community and collaboration in education

SUMMARY

Digital Hallways is an online platform that shares student and teacher work with the greater community designed for the Woodstock Union High School and Middle School in Vermont. We proposed a public landing page that allowed community members to easily access student and teacher portfolios, as well as the ability to contact school district members to partner with classes to provide enriching learning experiences for students.

ROLE

Digital Hallways was created as part of a group project for the Senior Design Challenge (SDC) course at Dartmouth, a capstone project for the Human-Centered Design minor. I worked collaboratively with two other students (Mia Drury and Giovanna Boyle) to conduct user research, brainstorm solutions, implement strategy guidelines, and design the program experience and digital interfaces.
